About Nevin Kadlec

Full Stack Developer & Business Problem Solver

I'm a passionate full-stack developer with over 25 years of programming experience, specializing in creating practical technology solutions for small businesses. My journey began at age 8 when my mother took me to her college computer course, sparking a lifelong fascination with how computers work.

Through blue collar jobs, trucking, and formal education, I've maintained programming as a constant passion - always exploring new languages, tools, and technologies. After a dangerous trucking incident in winter 2023, I decided to transform this decades-long hobby into a professional career.

Based in Fargo, North Dakota, I now help businesses automate manual processes and improve efficiency through custom applications. My unique combination of technical expertise, real-world work experience, and genuine understanding of business operations allows me to create solutions that actually solve problems and provide lasting value.
