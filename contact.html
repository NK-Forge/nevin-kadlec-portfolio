Common Questions

Quick answers to help you understand how we can work together

Why do you prefer email over phone calls?

Email communication ensures we capture all project details accurately, creates a clear paper trail for reference, and allows me to provide thoughtful, comprehensive responses. This leads to better project outcomes and fewer misunderstandings.

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

I typically respond within 24 hours, often much faster. My responses are thorough and include follow-up questions, initial ideas, and clear next steps for your project.

Do you work with businesses outside North Dakota?

Absolutely! I work remotely with small businesses nationwide. Email communication and modern development tools make location irrelevant for delivering excellent results.

What information should I include in my initial email?

The more details, the better! Describe your current challenges, how you handle processes now, your goals, timeline, and budget range. Don't worry about being too detailed - thorough information helps me provide better solutions.

Can you work within my budget?

I offer solutions at various price points and can often suggest phased approaches to fit different budgets. Let's discuss your needs and find an approach that works for your business and financial situation.

What happens after I contact you?

I'll respond with follow-up questions and initial ideas. If it seems like a good fit, I'll create a detailed proposal with timeline, features, and fixed pricing. No pressure - just clear information to help you make the best decision.
